Hi! My name is Brian Chang, and welcome to my website.

I'm a senior at Diamond Bar High School in Diamond Bar, CA, about an hour's drive south of Los Angeles. I'm a News Editor at The Bull's Eye, my school paper, and the eSports Editor at Second Floor Sports.

I attended the Johns Hopkins Center for Talented Youth program for six years, taking a number of writing and science courses. I took Writing and Reading Workshop, Writing and Imagination, Heroes and Villains, Paleobiology, Logic: Principles of Reasoning and Genetics. I was a student in Cluster 3: Tissue and Tumor Biology and Mathematical Reasoning at the University of California, Irvine, in 2015 as part of the COSMOS program. In 2017 I attended the Medill-Northwestern Journalism Institute.
